Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10 July 2026

This policy explains how Ride ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses and protects your personal information when you use this website and join our pre-launch waitlist. We keep it short because we collect very little.

1. Who we are

Ride is a ride-hailing service preparing to launch in The Bahamas. For any privacy question or request, contact us at privacy@ride.bs.

2. What we collect

When you join the waitlist, we collect:

  • Your mobile number, as entered in the signup form
  • Your country, as selected in the phone field
  • The date and time you signed up

That is the complete list. We do not ask for your name, email address or any other personal details at this stage, and we do not collect precise location data from this website.

3. How we use it

We use your mobile number for one purpose: to send you a small number of text messages about the Ride launch, such as telling you when the service goes live in your area and how to download the app. We do not use your number for any other marketing, and we will not add you to other lists.

4. Legal basis and consent

We process your information on the basis of your consent, which you give by entering your number and pressing "Notify me". You can withdraw consent at any time (see section 8), after which we will delete your number from the waitlist.

5. Where your information is stored

Waitlist signups are stored securely in Google Workspace (Google Sheets), which may process data on servers outside The Bahamas. Google acts as our processor and does not use this data for its own purposes. Access is restricted to the small team preparing the Ride launch.

6. Who we share it with

We do not sell, rent or trade your personal information. We share it only with service providers who help us operate (such as Google, for storage, and an SMS provider when we send launch messages), and only to the extent needed to do so. We may also disclose information if required by law.

7. How long we keep it

We keep waitlist data until the Ride app has launched and launch communications are complete, after which the waitlist will be deleted. If you withdraw consent earlier, we delete your entry promptly.

8. Your rights

You can ask us at any time to:

  • Confirm what information we hold about you
  • Correct it if it is wrong
  • Delete it and remove you from the waitlist

Email privacy@ride.bs from any address, including the number you signed up with in the message, and we will act on your request within a reasonable time. These rights sit alongside those in the Data Protection (Privacy of Personal Information) Act of The Bahamas.

9. Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information, including access controls on the systems where it is stored and encrypted connections (HTTPS) on this website. No system is perfectly secure, but the small amount of data we hold limits the risk to you.

10. Children

This website and waitlist are not directed at children under 18, and we do not knowingly collect information from them. If you believe a child has joined the waitlist, contact us and we will remove the entry.

11. Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, we will post the updated version on this page with a new "last updated" date. Significant changes affecting how we use your number will be communicated before they take effect.
